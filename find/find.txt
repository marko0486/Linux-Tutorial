Find empty files | folders
find -empty -type d
find -empty -type f

Find the files and folders of a specific user | Do not find files and folders of a specific user 
find ! -user <user> -ls
find -user <user> -ls

Find all the .txt files that were modified between days and make a backup
find -name "*.txt" -newermt 2021-04-27 ! -newermt 2021-04-28 -exec tar -cvzf Backup/MyBackup.tar.gz {} \;

What is the size of the files
by date  find . -type f -newermt 2021-04-27 -exec du -ch {} +
by user  find . -type f -user hugo -exec du -ch {} +
